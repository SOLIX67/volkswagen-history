VELOCITI & ROX

The Ultimate Citi Golf Expressions

Citi Golf Velociti

The Spirit of Velociti

Launched in 2005, the Velociti was Volkswagen South Africa's answer to enthusiasts demanding a proper performance Citi Golf. It directly channeled the spirit of the original GTI with its 1.8i fuel-injected engine producing 82 kW.

Key Features:

  • Engine: 1.8L 8V fuel-injected (82 kW)
  • Wheels: 15" "Velociti" alloy wheels
  • Brakes: Red-painted calipers
  • Exterior: Subtle rear spoiler, Velociti badging
  • Interior: Sport seats, leather steering wheel
  • Performance: 0-100 km/h in 9.5 seconds

Citi Golf Rox

The Lifestyle Statement

Introduced as a limited edition model, the Rox targeted the youth market with its vibrant styling and premium features. It was as much about fashion and lifestyle as it was about motoring.

Key Features:

  • Colors: Rox Red, Snow White, and other vibrant options
  • Wheels: 15" "Citi Rox" alloy wheels
  • Exterior: Contrasting black roof, side stripes
  • Interior: Color-coded seats, premium audio system
  • Styling: Youth-focused, fashion-forward design
  • Market: Limited edition lifestyle model
